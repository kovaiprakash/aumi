High-Efficiency Power Transformers

Engineered for reliability, thermal stability and efficient energy conversion for modern power electronics and electric mobility applications.

Product Portfolio

AuMi manufactures low and medium power transformers optimized for modern electronics, industrial automation, power conversion and electric mobility applications. Our transformer manufacturing capability supports products from 10 W up to 2 kW using precision CNC winding technology.


The portfolio includes industrial isolation transformers, control transformers, inverter transformers, high-frequency SMPS magnetics, and specialty magnetics for advanced electronics systems.

Transformer Categories

Isolation Transformers

Electrical isolation transformers used in medical equipment, industrial panels and UPS systems.

Power Range: 100 VA – 2 kVA

Control Transformers

Compact transformers used in PLC panels, CNC machines, relay circuits and industrial automation systems.

Power Range: 50 VA – 1 kVA

Inverter Transformers

Low-frequency transformers for home inverters, solar hybrid systems and UPS power systems.

Power Range: 500 W – 2 kW

SMPS Transformers

High-frequency ferrite transformers designed for switch-mode power supplies and EV charging systems.

Power Range: 20 W – 2 kW

Toroidal Transformers

Low-noise high-efficiency toroidal transformers used in audio equipment, medical electronics and precision systems.

Power Range: 50 VA – 2 kVA

Inductors & Chokes

Magnetic components including line chokes, DC inductors and EMI filter magnetics for power electronics.

Power Range: Up to 1 kW systems
