About AuMi

AuMi Robotics and Mobility Solutions (ARMS) delivers innovative industrial automation and electric mobility solutions. We specialize in the development of advanced EV control systems—including inverters, Battery Management Systems (BMS), and DC–DC converters—backed by deep expertise in embedded software, power electronics, automotive systems engineering and AUTOSAR Base-software.

AuMi Robotics and Mobility Solutions (ARMS) is an engineering-focused company dedicated to industrial automation and electric mobility technologies. Our core offerings include the design and development of electric vehicle control units such as inverters, Battery Management Systems (BMS), and DC–DC converters.

With strong capabilities in embedded software development across diverse microcontroller architectures, automotive communication protocols (CAN, Ethernet, I²C, SPI), power electronics, and system-level automotive engineering, ARMS delivers reliable, scalable, and future-ready solutions for modern mobility and industrial applications.

Our Expertise Includes:

  • Industrial Automation Solutions
  • Electric Vehicle Control Units (Inverter, BMS, DC–DC Converter)
  • Embedded Software Development (Multi-MCU Architectures)
  • Communication Protocols: CAN, Ethernet, I²C, SPI
  • Power Electronics & Circuit Design
  • Automotive Systems Engineering
  • AUTOSAR Base-software
