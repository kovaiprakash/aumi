About AuMi

Engineering reliable, scalable solutions for electric mobility and industrial automation

AuMi Robotics and Mobility Solutions is an innovation-driven engineering company focused on developing advanced solutions in electric mobility, robotics, and intelligent embedded systems. The company specializes in building scalable, secure, and high-performance platforms for next-generation automotive and robotics applications.

The development team is led by highly experienced software, firmware, hardware, and power electronics professionals with over 20 years of proven expertise in the automotive industry. The leadership team has successfully managed and delivered complex vehicle programs while leading global engineering teams across India, Germany, China, and Japan.
