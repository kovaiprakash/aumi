About AuMi

AuMi Robotics and Mobility Solutions develops advanced technologies for electric mobility and industrial automation. Our engineering expertise spans embedded systems, power electronics, and automotive control systems. We specialize in the design and development of EV control units including inverters, battery management systems, DC‑DC converters, and intelligent automation platforms.

With deep expertise in embedded software development across multiple microcontroller architectures, automotive communication protocols (CAN, Ethernet, I²C, SPI), power electronics, and system-level automotive engineering, AuMi delivers reliable, scalable, and future-ready solutions for modern mobility and industrial applications. Our capabilities also include AUTOSAR-based software development, enabling robust and standardized automotive systems.
